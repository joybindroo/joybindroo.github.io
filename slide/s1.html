🔄 Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) Model

Sustainable Model for Bihar Health Department

Build:

  • 🛠️ Develop AI chatbot with Bhashini and ChatGPT
  • 📚 Integrate with Directory Services
  • 📡 Set up Health Information Dissemination systems

Operate:

  • 🏃‍♂️ Manage chatbot operations
  • 🧑‍🏫 Provide capacity-building support for health workers
  • 📊 Utilize cloud analytics for performance monitoring

Transfer:

  • 🤝 Gradual ownership transfer to Bihar's government IT team
  • 📚 Comprehensive training for self-reliance and sustainability
